Everything started when my mother met my father...

... quick fast-forward to a couple of years ago, when I realized I always loved to understand how the things are made to build them myself, I then decided that I wanted to learn a programming language in order to be able to build something from scratch. At the beginnig the easiest and most exciting thing was starting building games (you can see something really simple here) and after my first game-jam I fell in love with the magic of writing code and make things happen, so I moved from building games in C# to learn Python and finally build some software.

From there the slipping into web development was really a matter of time, after the back-end which satisfied my tecnical-skill-mind I landed on web design, which nourishes the amused-creative-child.

Now I dream of improving my skills and expanding my knowledge to deliver incredible products to my clients.

I travelled a lot and did many many different jobs in my life, from security and self defence teacher to youtube content creator about board-games. I always tried to absorb as many as possible from every field and every travel in order to have different points of view and be able to give something more in the next one.

Ecommerce clone

My first e-commerce clone was made during the course "CS50: Web Programming with Python and JS" and it allowed me to learn and deepen the understanding of databases and how to save and manipulate users entries.

Here users are able to load an article with a starting price and a deadline. Other users could add it to watchlist or place bids, receive a notification in case they won the auction and be the only ones to see the article they won.

Video Github

Mail service clone
