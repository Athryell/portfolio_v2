Eco-mmerce

An e-commerce clone

My first e-commerce clone was made during a django course and it allow me to learn and deepen the understanding of databases and how to save users entries.

Features:

  • Allow users to load their articles, set a starting price and a deadline.
  • Added a chance to close the auction at any moment before the deadline.
  • Other users could bet on the article (only a higher value)
  • They would receive a notification in case they won the bid and they would be able to see the closed auction
  • Added a “watchlist” page to keep track of interesting articles
  • Responsive design
